Ready to go all in?

Your spot isn't given. It's earned. Commit to 10 days of real work on your passion, submit your First Drop video, and GripTape backs you with everything we've got — funding, a Champion, 8 weeks, and a community of 4,000+ who've been right where you're standing.

Your First Drop video needs to answer these four things

01   Who are you and what's your passion?

What are you trying to bring to life and why does it matter to you?

Ex: My name is Taylor, and I am passionate about restaurant owners because I want to help people in my community tell their stories.

02   What did you accomplish in the past 10 days?

Tell us AND show us — your quilt, website, 3D model, recording, whatever you built.

03   Where are you headed next?

What do you want to learn or do over the next 8 weeks?

Ex: I plan to dive deeper into editing and storytelling. I will tell the stories of 5 restaurant owners in my community.

04   What's your Full Send goal by week 10?

What will you create, launch, or make real?

Ex: My end goal is to launch a club and have 10 pen pals operating between NY and Vietnam.
